Projects

Simple Chatbot for Students

Ideathon 2025 Team ID: 25AML55 Digital & Skill Development
Try Project 1 →

A rule-based chatbot built to give students instant, predictable answers to common queries — without the cost, complexity, or hallucination risk of a full conversational AI system. The idea was simple: most student questions are repetitive and well-defined, so a lightweight, keyword-driven bot can resolve them instantly, 24/7, while freeing up mentors and staff for the queries that actually need a human. Built as part of Ideathon 2025 under the Digital & Skill Development theme, in collaboration with Capgemini and Panimalar Engineering College's Institution's Innovation Council.
